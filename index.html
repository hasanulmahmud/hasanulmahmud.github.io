Biography

I design energy-efficient, low-latency deep neural networks for edge and cloud environments, with a focus on converting autoencoders for secure and performant inference under resource constraints. My work spans model compression, SLO-aware deployment, and adversarial robustness.


Research Interests

  • Energy-efficient and low-latency DNNs
  • Converting autoencoders & model compression
  • Edge AI and systems for ML
  • Adversarial robustness & security
  • Computer vision & deep learning
  • Parallel and distributed computing
